Trees

Birds

Sky

Air

Earth and nothingless is my very being

Spirit

So many things fight for my identity

My phone

Car

My watch on my wrist

Picking up my phone to write this that quick made me forget and fight to remember

My thoughts make me forget who I am

My judgements

My lack of being

The only way you can feel anxious is by being stuck in the future… all thats happening and will happen is now

All these other things wear and weigh me down
