- **Zane dentist ideas and locations and cost**
    - **Brushing teeth tips:**
        - Let Him Choose: Give him some say in the process. Let him pick out a toothbrush and a toothpaste. There are a ton of fun kids' toothbrushes and milder-flavored toothpastes out there that aren't minty.
        - Give Him a Job: Let him hold one toothbrush while you use a second one to do the actual cleaning. This gives him something to do and makes him feel like he's in control. You could also try the "your turn, my turn" approach.
        - Find a Comfy Position: Try different positions. It might be easier for him if he's sitting on your lap with his head resting on your body.
        - Stay Consistent: Even if it's a total fight, keep at it. Making it a consistent part of the daily routine is what's most important, and eventually, he'll get used to it.
    - **Dentist Locations:**
        - Destination Pediatric Dentistry (Round Rock): Different themed rooms like a "Beach House" and "Safari Lodge" to help kids relax. Focus on anxiety-free experience.
        - Georgetown Pediatric Dentistry: Comfortable, stress-free environment with TVs and video games. Offers sedation if needed.
        - Star Smiles Pediatric Dentistry (Georgetown): High-quality care in a "fun, inviting and educational environment."
        - Kids First Pediatric Dentistry (Round Rock): Movie theater theme. Kids can watch movies while getting teeth cleaned. Flavored toothpaste and "No Cavity" awards.
    - **Making the Dentist Fun:**
        - Bring a Comfort Item: Let him bring his favorite stuffed animal or blanket.
        - Offer a Reward: After the visit, have a special outing or get a small toy to celebrate his bravery (avoid candy).
    - **Out of Pocket Costs:**
        - Average Cost: $30-$60 without insurance for basic cleaning and fluoride. $75-$200 for cleaning and polish.
        - New Patient Specials: Some places offer ~$79 new patient specials including exam, x-rays, cleaning, and fluoride.
        - Call Ahead: Ask about pricing and insurance coverage before booking.
