If you can break free at the hardest times the easiest should be easier and you should know you can do anything through Christ who gives you strength

I sat here for minutes trying to understand what thought or idea actually made me come out of my veil of darkness… and eventually realized it was no thought or thing… it was an action then Jesus dropped into my mind through a thought, but the thought wasn’t the answer, he is… and always is… so the question is how do you activate Jesus? You:

- You do the opposite thing that your feelings are telling you to do right now because you know it’s right… he shows up down that path… but you first have to turn around and look back for the king to come running…
- It ain’t even about not wanting your feelings… it’s about wanting Jesus. It’s not about taking your problems to Jesus, it’s about leaving them at the door, showing up… (I think?)
