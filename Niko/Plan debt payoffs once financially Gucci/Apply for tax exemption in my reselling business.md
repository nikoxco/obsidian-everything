**How to Get It (TX example since you’re in Austin):**

1. Apply for a Texas Sales and Use Tax Permit
→ https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/permit/
2. Takes ~1–2 weeks.
3. Once approved, fill out exemption certificates for vendors (they provide the forms).
4. Use your LLC or sole prop info (your Home Studios setup should qualify).

💡 Use it even if you’re not flipping the gear — if you’re using it for production, it’s often considered tax exempt under media/creative services depending on state.
